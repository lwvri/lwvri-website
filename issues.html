Advocacy & Positions

Issues We Champion

LWVRI takes positions and advocates on key issues affecting democracy, civil rights, and quality of life in Rhode Island. Positions are developed through study, member consensus, and national League guidance.

🗳️
Voting Rights

Protecting and expanding ballot access for all eligible Rhode Island voters. We oppose restrictive measures like the SAVE Act, advocate for mail-in voting access, and promote fair election administration.

View Our Position →
🌿
Environment & Climate

Advocating for strong environmental protections, opposing rollbacks of clean water rules, and supporting climate action at state and federal levels. We filed comments on EPA clean water rulemaking.

View Our Position →
🏫
Civics Education

Supporting students' right to a quality civics education. LWVRI filed an amicus brief in Cook v. McKee establishing the powerful connection between civics education and voter participation in RI.

View Our Position →
📚
Library Funding

Actively pursuing improved funding for public libraries as essential community resources and pillars of informed citizenship. The Providence League monitors and advocates for library budgets.

View Our Position →
🔍
Open Government

Monitoring government transparency through our Observer Corps which tracks legislative proceedings and holds officials accountable. We advocate for open meeting laws and public records access.

View Our Position →
🔫
Gun Safety

Advocating for common-sense gun safety policies including background checks, safe storage laws, and red flag provisions to protect Rhode Island communities.

View Our Position →
🏛️
Redistricting

Ensuring fair, transparent redistricting that accurately represents Rhode Island's diverse communities. We oppose partisan gerrymandering and advocate for independent, criteria-based map-drawing.

View Our Position →
👩‍⚕️
Women's Health

Defending reproductive rights and women's healthcare access. LWVRI organizes advocacy actions and community programs around women's bodily autonomy and healthcare policy changes.

View Our Position →
🤝
Immigration

Supporting welcoming policies for immigrants and refugees in Rhode Island and opposing federal actions that threaten established immigrant communities. We co-sponsor local support programs.

View Our Position →
